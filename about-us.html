Frequently Asked Questions


Is the food that's discounted and shown in the Zilch app safe to eat?

All the food that Zilch shows is as safe to eat as if you walk into the grocery store regularly to eat. Zilch simply publicizes discounts that restaurants have on their food. Usually, the discounts are for food items whose ingredients are approaching their use-by dates, but it is still 100% fresh before and on that date.

Can I use discount coupons on top of Zilch discounts?

It is up to the store's discretion. Some grocery stores will let you compound the discounts, while others may limit you to use one discount. It would be best to contact the restaurant regarding whether or not you can do this.

How can I find out if Zilch is in my area?

Zilch constantly updates the restaurants that it partners with. To find out if any of these are in your area, click on the Locations tab above, or click here.

How do I use Zilch?

You can use Zilch through an Android or iOS device by downloading the app. First create an account. Then follow the steps to subscribe to local grocery stores. Whenever you login, you will be able to see ongoing discounts for those stores. If you have the app, you will also be able to enable push notifications for stores of special interest. Then, when you go to the store, show the discount item's code to the cashier, and he/she will automatically factor the discount into your bill.
