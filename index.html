What We Do

Zilch wants to help customers find great deals, enable grocery stores to sell the entirety of their inventory and generate greater sales, and aid environmental protection by preventing the waste of food and land pollution.

Cheaper Prices

By using Zilch, members can check whether there are any available discounts in their local grocery stores.

Less Food Wasted

Zilch encourages grocery stores to offer discounts when they need to deplete their inventory, allowing more items to be consumed instead of being wasted.

Something New

Zilch offers a new style of shopping. When in need of grocery items, look up which stores near you have discounts, and choose where to shop this way.

Partnerships

Zilch is currently available in Northern California. We partner with various local grocery stores to provide benefits for both the customers as well as our corporate partners.
